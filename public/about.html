Our Manifesto

We believe in a world where data empowers individuals and communities, not corporations. We're building a business intelligence tool that puts privacy first, keeps data local, and leverages AI to enhance human understanding, not replace it.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by Clara Bennett, DataWise was born from a frustration with existing BI tools. Clara, a data scientist with over a decade of experience, saw a gap in the market for a solution that respected user privacy while providing powerful analytical capabilities. Her vision was to create a tool that could be used by anyone, regardless of their technical expertise, to gain insights from their data without compromising their personal information.

Our Values

Privacy-First

We prioritize your data privacy above all else. Your data stays with you, always.

Local-First

Your data is processed locally on your device, ensuring maximum security and control.

Human-Centered AI

AI should augment human intelligence, not replace it. We build tools that empower you to make better decisions.

Join us in building a more ethical and empowering future for data.
